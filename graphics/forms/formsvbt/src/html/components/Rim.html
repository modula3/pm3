Rim (filter)

Displays a rim around its child. A rim is like a border, but uses the background color rather than the foreground color.

Pen
(Real, 1.0)
The thickness of the rim.
Texture
(Text)
The name of a pixmap-resource used for the border's texture, which defaults to Pixmap.Solid.
Shape
The shape of its child, plus twice the value of Pen in each dimension.
