General purpose graphical libraries

General purpose graphical libraries from low level X11 access to high level widgets and 3D graphics.

X11
Modula-3 wrappers for X11.
Motif
Modula-3 wrappers for Motif on platforms supporting it.
OpenGL
Modula-3 wrappers for OpenGL, based on OpenGL or Mesa, depending on platforms.
PEX
Modula-3 wrappers for PEX. Newer systems are generally built with OpenGL.
Trestle (ui)
A windowing toolkit for X11 and Win32.
VBTkit
High level widgets for Trestle.
jvideo
Support for the DEC JVideo video system.
videoVBT
Widget for video display.
images
Simple image manipulation library.
fisheye
An experiment in displaying graphs with a fish eye view.
