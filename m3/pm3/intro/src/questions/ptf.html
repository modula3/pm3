Can I get Modula-3 other than by FTP or HTTP?

Prime Time Freeware (PTF) includes Modula-3. PTF is a set of two ISO-9660 CDroms filled with 3GB of freeware, issued semi-annually. PTF is distributed via bookstores and mail. You can reach PTF using: 


        Email:  ptf@cfcl.com
        Fax:    [1] (408) 738 2050
        Voice:  [1] (408) 738 4832
        Mail:   Prime Time Freeware
                415-112 N. Mary Ave., Suite 50
                Sunnyvale, CA 94086
                USA

Many Linux CDroms include a copy of the FTP site tsx-11.mit.edu which has Linux binaries for Modula-3.
